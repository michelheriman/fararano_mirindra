FARARANO MIRINDRA

"Fararano" means the end of Rain season in Malagasy. It refers to the harvest period.

Our solution is straightforward: Farmers can easily input details about their current crops through our user-friendly app. This data is then aggregated into our comprehensive online platform, where it is transformed into a Business Intelligence (BI)-driven dashboard. This powerful tool enables private sector stakeholders to make informed decisions based on real-time insights. Additionally, we offer tailored recommendations for smallholder farmers, leveraging advanced technologies such as remote sensing, agrometeorology, and market analytics to optimize crop production.

In essence, our network of smallholder farmers serves as our valued suppliers, and our goal is to empower them with the tools and knowledge to maximize yield and successfully connect with the market.
